About Drav / Pome Drav

History
Drav Country was established on 12th April, 2019. It was originally called "Dav" Country comprising of the three union leaders who decided to partake in forming one great nation; namely-
  • Meutresme.Dhriti Asrani (D)
  • Meutreme.Aarush Chhabria(A)
  • Meutresme.Vihaa Asrani (V)

After the addition of "The costerio enclave", Meutreme.Ronit Chhabria became the nominal name holder; forming the complete name-Drav. Today Drav is home to diverse groups of people, both culturally and financially. The Dravian government was established to ensure that all people, coming from different backgrounds, feel safe under one nation and identity, "Dravians".

Let us now take a look at the form of government.-

     THE GOVERNMENT
Drav has the "Constituional Monarchy" form of government. However, in our government "The Queen" does not wield any power. By the quuen's proclamation on 12th May,2020, all of the ceremonial and any left governance-related powers were transferred to the office of "The Governor General".
The governor general represents the quuen at the Bundeshlann and carries some important duties such as:
  • Swearing-in of the vice-president and all senate ministers
  • Stamping all laws passed by the Sjlann including the national budget
  • Acting as a vital link between government and crown
  • Chairing the "Drav council", to check on the constitutional validity of all laws in effect or to be in effect
  • Calling upon the core cabinet to discuss any discrepancies in the law(s), found by the council.
  • Presenting to court, any unconstitutional law(s) to protect the rights and duties of dravians


After the Governor general comes the most important post-"The President". He/she is elected, not directly but by a two-vote system that ensures, every voice is heard in parliament. The party with the majority in parliament goes on to form the government. This usually conists of the President, the vice-president and then the cabinet ministers. They inturn, form the core cabinet. Outside the core cabinet is the "Utcabinete" or the out cabinet which comprises of the some ministersof government who hold not-so-important portfolios as the core cabinet; they part of the government nonetheless.
The job of the Vice-President is to assist the president and fill his/her role in their abscence. They also advise and chair high-level intra-cabinet committees whcih serve as an esential gear system to keep the government running smoothly.
